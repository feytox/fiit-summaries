1) $f(x_{1} + x_{2}, y_{1}) = f(x_{1}, y_{1}) + f(x_{2}, y_{1})$ 
2) $f(\lambda x_{1}, y_{1}) = \lambda f(x_{1}, y_{1})$
3) $f(x_{1}, y_{1} + y_{2}) = f(x_{1}, y_{1}) + f(x_{1}, y_{2})$
4) $f(x_{1},\lambda y_{1}) = \lambda f(x_{1}, y_{1})$
